# Design 05 — Launch, Live-Ops, and Community

**Last updated:** 2026-06-04  
**Design canon** — operate sustainably before attempting a large live service.  
**Related:** [liveops/retention.md](../liveops/retention.md) · [design/03-monetization.md](../design/03-monetization.md)

---

## 1. Operating principle

Launch a polished authored game with repeatable modes. Live operations **support** quality; they do not replace it.

```
stable build → clear onboarding → fair monetization → useful analytics
  → small repeatable events → authored expansion
```

---

## 2. Launch stages

| Stage | Audience | Goal |
|-------|----------|------|
| Internal prototype | Team + trusted testers | Prove controls and combat |
| Closed alpha | Invited players | Validate Khan 1 replayability |
| Closed beta | Broader testers | Progression, saves, devices |
| Soft launch | Limited audience | Retention, fairness, revenue |
| Global release | Public | Trust and momentum |
| Post-launch | Active players | Sustain community and quality content |

---

## 3. Sustainable content cadence

**Weekly:** validated Daily Tale seeds, one weekly challenge, bug triage, one community post, support review.

**Monthly:** one small cosmetic collection or curated bundle, one community challenge, quality update if needed, accessibility review, balance review from data + playtests.

**Quarterly / milestone:** one themed event, one meaningful content update or progress report, store creative refresh, roadmap review.

Do not promise weekly handcrafted maps or expensive animation sets unless proven deliverable.

## 5. Community strategy

Share: real gameplay clips, asset-to-gameplay comparisons, tower silhouette iterations, boss telegraph breakdowns, art-direction notes, accessibility improvements, challenge runs, respectful Shahnameh context, player builds, patch notes.

Invite feedback from Persian-speaking players and cultural reviewers; treat corrections respectfully.

---

## 6. Analytics dashboard

**Product health:** DAU/WAU, sessions, length, crash-free rate, load failures, tutorial completion, Khan funnel, replay after win/defeat.

**Gameplay health:** tower/upgrade choices, hero usage, Fate/relic picks, objectives, corruption-collapse causes, hijack recovery, boss failure reasons, large-map camera friction.

**Business health:** store views, conversion, revenue by product, Supporter Pack, rewarded-ad opt-in, ad removal, refunds, entitlement support, review sentiment.

---

## 7. First 90 days after release

| Window | Actions |
|--------|---------|
| Days 1–14 | Monitor crashes/support daily; fix blockers; track onboarding and Khan 1 funnel; respond to reviews |
| Days 15–30 | Quality patch; tutorial friction; store clarity if needed; one simple community challenge |
| Days 31–60 | First small event; refresh store with real gameplay; evaluate Daily Tale and Endless |
| Days 61–90 | Roadmap update; decide on authored expansion; curated cosmetic collection; confirm sustainable cadence |

---

## 8. Support priority

1. Purchase or entitlement failure  
2. Save loss  
3. Crash or progression blocker  
4. Severe performance regression  
5. Unfair balance exploit  
6. Cosmetic or presentation bug  

Maintain: support/privacy contacts, known issues, patch notes, save recovery, purchase-entitlement recovery, rollback plan, ability to disable broken offers/events.

---

## 9. Live-ops stop rule

Reduce live-ops scope when it harms core quality. Fewer excellent events beat many rushed ones. Growth still depends on sincere player recommendation.
