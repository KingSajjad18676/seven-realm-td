README 03 — ETHICAL MONETIZATION AND BUSINESS ROADMAP
Build Revenue by Creating Trust, Not Frustration
1. BUSINESS PRINCIPLE
A successful game earns money because players value it.
The store should communicate:
This game respects my time.
The battle is fair.
The art is special.
I understand what I am buying.
Spending supports the game.
I do not need to pay to win.
The game must never create artificial pain merely to sell the cure.
2. RECOMMENDED MODEL
Use a fair free-to-start model.
Core structure:
free access to the early game
strong playable Khan 1 experience
fair campaign progression
optional cosmetics
Founder’s Supporter Pack
permanent ad-removal purchase
limited optional rewarded ads only after testing
future authored expansions after demand is proven
Do not launch with a complex battle pass, subscription, or large live-service economy.
